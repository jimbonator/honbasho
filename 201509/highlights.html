Aki 2015 (September) Grand Sumo Highlights

Highlight 01: 二日目 day 白鵬 嘉風* (引き落とし) 横綱白鵬がまさかの２連敗、大腿四頭筋腱炎の為、３日目から休場し、横綱になって初めて４９場所目で休場、幕内連続二桁勝利は５１場所で止まった。

2nd day Hakuho Yoshikaze* (hikiotoshi) Yokozuna Hakuho surprisingly suffers two straight losses. Due to an inflammation in his left knee, he withdraws from the tournament on the third day. It is his first time missing out on a tournament since becoming a Yokozuna, withdrawing from his 49th tournament. His consecutive double-digit wins in the Makuuchi Division end at 51 tournaments.

Highlight 02: 三日目 day 嘉風* 鶴竜 (押し出し) 嘉風が横綱鶴竜を破り二日連続で金星を獲得した。

3rd day Yoshikaze* Kakuryu (oshidashi) Yoshikaze defeats Yokozuna Kakuryu, and attains a kinboshi two days in a row.

Highlight 03: 十一日目 day 照ノ富士* 琴奨菊 (寄り倒し) 大関照ノ富士が無傷の１１連勝、年間勝利は５５勝となり白鵬を抜いてトップとなる。

11th day Terunofuji* Kotoshogiku (yoritaoshi) Terunofuji is undefeated for 11 straight wins, and with 55 wins this year he has surpassed Hakuho and is now at the lead.

Highlight 04: 十一日目 day 栃煌山 鶴竜* (叩き込み) 十日目、妙義龍に敗れ２敗となり後の無い横綱鶴竜は叩き込みで栃煌山を破る。

11th day Tochiozan Kakuryu* (hatakikomi) Yokozuna Kakuryu suffers his second loss to Myogiryu on the tenth day, but manages to stay in the game by defeating Tochiozan.

Highlight 05: 十二日目 day 照ノ富士 栃煌山* (寄り切り) 全勝の照ノ富士に土、１敗で照ノ富士、２敗は鶴竜、勢、３敗は稀勢の里、豊ノ島となる。

12th day Terunofuji Tochiozan* (yorikiri) Terunofuji's clean record is put to an end, and he now has one loss. Behind him are Kakuryu and Ikioi with two losses, followed by Kisenosato and Toyonoshima with three losses.

Highlight 06: 十三日目 day 栃ノ心* 勢 (寄り切り) 勢が栃ノ心に敗れ３敗となる。

13th day Tochinoshin* Ikioi (yorikiri) Ikioi is defeated by Tochinoshin and suffers his third loss.

Highlight 07: 十三日目 day 照ノ富士 稀勢の里* (寄り倒し) 照ノ富士が敗れ２敗となり、稀勢の里は３敗をキープする。

13th day Terunofuji Kisenosato* (yoritaoshi) Terunofuji suffers his second loss, and Kisenosato defends his three-loss count.

Highlight 08: 十三日目 day 豪栄道 鶴竜* (寄り切り) 横綱鶴竜が豪栄道を下し、２敗は鶴竜と照ノ富士、３敗で稀勢の里と勢となる。

13th day Goeido Kakuryu* (yorikiri) Yokozuna Kakuryu puts Goeido down; now at two losses are Kakuryu and Terunofuji, at three losses are Kisenosato and Ikioi.

Highlight 09: 十四日目 day 勢 琴奨菊* (押し出し) ３敗の勢が敗れ、４敗となり優勝争いから脱落する。

14th day Ikioi Kotoshogiku* (oshidashi) Ikioi suffers his fourth loss, and is now out of the competition.

Highlight 10: 十四日目 day 照ノ富士 豪栄道* (寄り切り) ２敗の照ノ富士が豪栄道に敗れ、千秋楽に本割りで勝ち決定戦に持ち込みたい。

14th day Terunofuji Goeido* (yorikiri) With two losses already, Terunofuji is defeated by Goeido. He'll need to win his scheduled bouts of the Senshuraku to make it to the championships.

Highlight 11: 十四日目 day 稀勢の里 鶴竜* (寄り倒し) 鶴竜は稀勢の里を破り２敗を守る。千秋楽に本割りで勝ち優勝を決めたい。

14th day Kisenosato Kakuryu* (yoritaoshi) Kakuryu defeats Kisenosato and defends his two loss count, hoping to win his scheduled bout of the Senshuraku to claim the championship.

Highlight 12: 千秋楽 day 勢* 阿夢露 (寄り切り) 勢が阿夢露を破り１１勝を挙げ、３度目の敢闘賞となる。

15th day Ikioi* Amuru (yorikiri) Ikioi defeats Amuru for his 11th win, and is awarded his third Fighting Spirit Prize.

Highlight 13: 千秋楽 day 栃ノ心* 豊ノ島 (寄り切り) 栃ノ心が千秋楽１０勝を挙げ５度目の敢闘賞に選ばれた。

15th day Tochinoshin* Toyonoshima (yorikiri) Tochinoshin claims his tenth victory in the Senshuraku, and wins his fifth Fighting Spirit Prize.

Highlight 14: 千秋楽 day 照ノ富士* 鶴竜 (寄り切り) 照ノ富士が勝って相星となり決定戦へ。

15th day Terunofuji* Kakuryu (yorikiri) Terunofuji claims victory, and together they face the championship playoff with even records.

Highlight 15: 千秋楽 day 鶴竜* 照ノ富士 (上手出し投げ) (優勝決定戦)

15th day Kakuryu* Terunofuji (uwatedashinage) (play-off)
