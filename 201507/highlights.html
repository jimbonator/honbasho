Nagoya 2015 (July) Grand Sumo Highlights

Highlight 01: 六日目 day 佐田の海 照ノ富士* (掬い投げ) 新大関の照ノ富士が初日からここまで6連勝とした。

6th day Sadanoumi Terunofuji* (sukuinage) The new Ozeki Terunofuji counts six straight wins from Day 1.

Highlight 02: 九日目 day 栃煌山* 鶴竜 (押し出し) ここまで1敗の関脇栃煌山が全勝の横綱鶴竜を破る。

9th day Tochiozan* Kakuryu (oshidashi) Sekiwake Tochiozan, at one loss so far, wins against the undefeated Yokozuna Kakuryu.

Highlight 03: 十日目 day 白鵬 栃煌山* (叩き込み) 栃煌山が二日連続で全勝の横綱を破る。1敗は白鵬、鶴竜、照ノ富士、栃煌山の4人。

10th day Hakuho Tochiozan* (hatakikomi) Tochiozan is victorious against an undefeated Yokozuna two days in a row.

Highlight 04: 十一日目 day 栃煌山 豪栄道* (首投げ) 大関豪栄道が首投げで1敗の関脇栃煌山を破る。

11th day Tochiozan Goeido* (kubinage) Ozeki Goeido wins against one loss count Sekiwake Tochiozan with a kubinage.

Highlight 05: 十一日目 day 白鵬* 照ノ富士 (寄り切り) 1敗同士の対戦、横綱白鵬に軍配が上がる。白鵬は51場所連続で2桁勝利。

11th day Hakuho* Terunofuji (yorikiri) In a battle between those with one loss each, Yokozuna Hakuho is declared the winner.

Highlight 06: 十一日目 day 琴奨菊 鶴竜* (寄り切り) 横綱鶴竜が大関琴奨菊を破り1敗は両横綱だけに。

11th day Kotoshogiku Kakuryu* (yorikiri) Yokozuna Kakuryu defeats Ozeki Kotoshogiku, and now the only ones left at one loss are the two Yokozunas.

Highlight 07: 十二日目 day 照ノ富士 鶴竜* (掬い投げ) 新大関の照ノ富士を鶴竜が破り1敗をキープ。照ノ富士は3敗と後退する。

12th day Terunofuji Kakuryu* (sukuinage) The new Ozeki Terunofuji is defeated by Kakuryu as he keeps his loss count at one.

Highlight 08: 十二日目 day 白鵬* 琴奨菊 (肩透かし) 白鵬も大関琴奨菊を破り両横綱が1敗を守る。

12th day Hakuho* Kotoshogiku (katasukashi) Hakuho also defeats Ozeki Kotoshogiku, and both Yokozunas are still at one loss each.

Highlight 09: 十三日目 day 白鵬* 豪栄道 (寄り切り) 白鵬が豪栄道を破り、1敗を守る。

13th day Hakuho* Goeido (yorikiri) Hakuho defeats Goeido, and defends his one loss count.

Highlight 10: 十三日目 day 稀勢の里* 鶴竜 (寄り切り) 1敗の鶴竜が大関稀勢の里に寄り切りで敗れ2敗になり、白鵬が再び単独トップになる。

13th day Kisenosato* Kakuryu (yorikiri) Kakuryu suffers his second loss as he is defeated by Ozeki Kisenosato.

Highlight 11: 十四日目 day 豪栄道 鶴竜* (寄り切り) 鶴竜は大関豪栄道を破り2敗を守る。千秋楽に白鵬を破り決定戦へと持ち込みたいところ。

14th day Goeido Kakuryu* (yorikiri) Kakuryu defeats Ozeki Goeido and keeps his loss count at two.

Highlight 12: 十四日目 day 白鵬* 稀勢の里 (寄り切り) 白鵬も稀勢の里を破り1敗を守り、明日勝てば35度目の優勝となる。

14th day Hakuho* Kisenosato (yorikiri) Hakuho defeats Kisenosato and defends his one loss count.

Highlight 13: 千秋楽 day 栃ノ心* 旭天鵬 (寄り切り) 旭天鵬は栃ノ心に敗れ3勝12敗と振るわず、翌日引退を表明した。

15th day Tochinoshin* Kyokutenho (yorikiri) Kyokutenho is defeated by Tochinoshin, and finishes with 3 wins to 12 losses. He announced his retirement the following day.

Highlight 14: 千秋楽 day 栃煌山 嘉風* (押し出し) 栃煌山は殊勲賞2度目、嘉風は敢闘賞4度目と三賞を共に受賞した力士の対戦となった。軍配は嘉風に上がり12勝3敗とした。栃煌山は10勝5敗。

15th day Tochiozan Yoshikaze* (oshidashi) Tochiozan is awarded his second Outstanding Performance Award, and Yoshikaze is awarded his fourth Fighting Spirit Prize. It has become a battle between Sansho awarded rikishi. The referee's fan is raised to Yoshikaze's favor as he scores 12 wins and 3 losses. Tochiozan is at 10 wins and 5 losses.

Highlight 15: 千秋楽 day 白鵬* 鶴竜 (寄り切り) 鶴竜が白鵬を破れば決定戦。しかし、白鵬は14勝1敗で35度目の優勝を果たす。

15th day Hakuho* Kakuryu (yorikiri) If Kakuryu defeats Hakuho, there will be a championship match. However, Hakuho finishes with 14 wins and 1 loss, claiming his 35th championship.
